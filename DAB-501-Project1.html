3) One plot displaying information about both a continuous variable and a categorical variable.

ggplot(data=CreditCard, mapping = aes(x =selfemp , y =income,fill=selfemp)) + 
      geom_boxplot() +
  theme_bw()+scale_fill_discrete(name="Self-Employed",
                         breaks=c("no", "yes"),
                         labels=c("no", "yes"))+
  labs(x="Self-Employed",y="Income(in USD10,000)",title = "Yearly income of customers who are self employed",caption = "Data from Creditcard dataset")+
  theme_grey()

In what ways do you think data visualization is important to understanding a data set?

Data visualization is very vital and important tools for understanding the data effectively by presenting data inform easier to understand , highlighting the trends and by removing unwanted data from data set. So the data visualization is important to interpret more complicated data.

In what ways do you think data visualization is important to communicating important aspects of a data set?

In a complex table data, it is hard for humans to look at uncountable data and make the meaning out of it. Data visualization makes it easier to look at such data by giving visual context or graphs. This makes it easier for human mind to comprehend data by elimination unnecessary data and makes it easier to identify trends and patterns.

What role does your integrity as an analyst play when creating a data visualization for communicating results to others?

As an data analyst integrity is one of the most important quality of analyst because when dealing with sensitive information one must not manipulate data and should be presented in given form because if not done so we might have an inaccurate data visualization and might have impact on our decision.
